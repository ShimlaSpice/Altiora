About This Expedition

The Spiti Valley Deep Run is the pinnacle 4×4 expedition in our lineup — a 9-day assault on some of the most remote, high-altitude terrain on earth. You'll cross Rohtang Pass, traverse the Spiti River valley, and summit Kunzum Pass at 15,059 ft, all in a convoy of max 10 vehicles.

This isn't a road trip. It's a gauntlet. And completing it earns you +18,380 ft on your Altiora altitude profile — enough to push most users to Climber or Summiteer level.

Day-by-Day Itinerary
1
Manali — Arrival & Briefing
Meet your crew at our Manali basecamp. Vehicle checks, gear distribution, and expedition briefing. Overnight in Manali.
Altitude: 6,726 ft
2
Manali → Rohtang Pass → Gramphoo
First major pass crossing. Rohtang tops out at 13,058 ft — your first altitude milestone. Camp at Gramphoo.
Max altitude: 13,058 ft · Rohtang Pass
3
Gramphoo → Kaza (via Kunzum)
The crown jewel day. Kunzum Pass at 15,059 ft is where most photos happen. Descend into the Spiti valley to Kaza.
Max altitude: 15,059 ft · Kunzum Pass
4
Kaza — Exploration Day
Ki Monastery, Kibber village, and the highest petrol pump in Asia. Acclimatization day with optional Langza hike.
Altitude: 12,500 ft
5
Kaza → Pin Valley
Off-road detour into Pin Valley National Park — snow leopard territory. River crossings, unpaved tracks, raw landscape.
Altitude: 12,000–14,000 ft
6
Pin Valley → Tabo → Dhankar
Ancient Tabo monastery (996 AD). Dhankar Lake hike at 14,107 ft. Camp with panoramic valley views.
Max altitude: 14,107 ft · Dhankar Lake
7
Dhankar → Nako → Chitkul
Cross into the Kinnaur district. Nako Lake, apple orchards, and the last Indian village before the Tibet border.
Altitude: 11,300 ft
8
Chitkul → Sangla → Shimla Drive
Descend through the Baspa valley. Last mountain views before civilization returns. Evening reach Shimla.
Altitude: 7,200 ft
9
Shimla — Departure
Post-expedition debrief. Altitude score updated to your Altiora profile. +18,380 ft officially earned.
Total altitude earned: +18,380 ft
